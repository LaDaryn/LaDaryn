About Me

La Daryn grew up in Edmond, OK and received his BSAG from Oklahoma State University and spent four years working support in small animal veterinary care. La Daryn also has had experience volunteering and caring for large animals like horses and chimpanzees. He later learned skills in HTML, CSS and JavaScript to pursue a career in front-end development while working in veterinary practices and a Cargill feed distribution center in Oklahoma City.

La Daryn enjoys to "learn something new everyday" and to surround himself with teachers and skill master. Whether it's establishing himself or finishing that book he's been writing, La Daryn is his best self when he is moving with a goal in mind.
